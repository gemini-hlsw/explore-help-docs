# AGS Diameter

The AGS Diameter defines the circular region that must remain free of vignetting by the peripheral wavefront sensor arm. The Automatic Guide Star (AGS) service selects the optimal guide star while ensuring that the arm does not obstruct any part of this region. The AGS Diameter does not specify the allowable distance of the guide star from the science target; rather, it specifies the size of the unvignetted field that must be preserved while positioning the guide probe.
