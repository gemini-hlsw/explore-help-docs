## Requested Coordinates + Configurations + Constraints

This tile provides a comprehensive view of all unique combinations of target coordinates, instrument configurations, and observation constraints in the program.
This list is initially populated from observations requested in the proposal, and the "Status" column reflects the TACs decision:  🟢 = "Approved" and 🔴 = "Denied."  

If a new set of CCC is needed, one may construct an observation using the new CCC, either from scratch or by modifying an existing observation, 
and the new CCC will appear in the table of "Unrequested Coordinates + Configurations + Constraints" below.
CCCs in that table may be requested (additional help available at the top of that table),
and the new CCC will appear in this table with the status 🟡 = "Pending" until the request can be reviewed by the Head of Science Operations.

The "Obs" column lists observations that correspond to the CCC in that row.
Observations that use approved CCCs may be marked as Ready, whereas observations with unapproved CCCs may not.

Buttons at the top right allow viewing request justifications and managing CCC requests.

For a detailed explanation of what defines a unique CCC, refer to the help link at the top of the Unrequested CCC table.
